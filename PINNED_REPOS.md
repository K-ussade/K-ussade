## Pinning Rules

- Use six pins maximum.
- Do not pin private or sealed repositories.
- Do not pin empty repositories without boundary-aware README files.
- Do not pin repos whose descriptions imply live models, datasets, Spaces, deployments, schools, NEURONAs, services, trading systems, performance, or client outcomes.
- Keep Hugging Face artifacts marked `planned` until public artifacts, cards or READMEs, release notes, safety notes, companion links, and review status exist.
- Keep portfolio and Upwork relevance marked future/review-required until Alexandra approves publication language and supporting evidence.
